# Preferences

Concrete preferences the user has expressed about coding, tooling, and workflow. Each entry should cite where it came from (a raw source or a dated in-conversation observation in [[log]]).

> _Empty on purpose. Fill in as preferences emerge. Prefer concrete, actionable entries over vague ones — "Uses pytest with `-x` flag" is useful; "Likes testing" is not._
